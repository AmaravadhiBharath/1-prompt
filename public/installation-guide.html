1. Requirements

Requirement Details
Browser Google Chrome (version 116 or later) or any Chromium-based browser (Edge, Brave, Arc, Opera)
Operating System Windows, macOS, Linux, or ChromeOS
Account Google account (optional - required only for cloud sync and history features)

2. Install from Chrome Web Store

1
Open the Chrome Web Store

Visit the 1-prompt listing on the Chrome Web Store:

chrome web store / 1-prompt

2
Click "Add to Chrome"

Click the blue "Add to Chrome" button on the listing page.

A confirmation dialog will appear showing the permissions the extension needs. Click "Add extension" to confirm.

3
Installation complete

You'll see a notification confirming 1-prompt has been added. The welcome page will open automatically to guide you through setup.

3. Pin to Toolbar

Pinning 1-prompt to your toolbar gives you one-click access to the side panel.

1
Click the Extensions (puzzle) icon

In the top-right corner of Chrome, click the puzzle piece icon to open the extensions menu.

2
Pin 1-prompt

Find 1-prompt in the list and click the pin icon next to it. The 1-prompt icon will now appear permanently in your toolbar.

Tip: Once pinned, simply click the 1-prompt icon in your toolbar to open the side panel on any supported AI platform.

4. Sign In (Optional)

Signing in with Google unlocks additional features but is not required for basic use.

Feature Without Sign-In With Sign-In

To sign in, click the "Continue with Google" button on the welcome page or in the side panel. You can also choose "Continue without signing in" to use 1-prompt as a guest.

5. Your First Capture

1
Navigate to a supported AI platform

Open any supported AI chat platform (e.g., chatgpt.com, claude.ai, or gemini.google.com).

2
Have a conversation

Chat with the AI as you normally would. Send one or more prompts.

3
Open the 1-prompt side panel

Click the 1-prompt icon in your toolbar, or use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + Shift + E (Windows/Linux) or Cmd + Shift + E (Mac).

4
Extract your prompts

Click the "Capture" button in the side panel. 1-prompt will extract all your prompts (user messages only) from the current conversation.

Your prompts will appear in the side panel, ready to copy, edit, or compile.

Note: 1-prompt captures prompts in real time as you send them. You can also extract all prompts from an existing conversation at any time.

6. Compile Your Prompts

The Compile feature uses AI to merge all your prompts from a conversation into a single, cohesive instruction.

1
Capture your prompts

Follow the steps above to capture prompts from a conversation.

2
Click "Compile"

Click the "Compile" button. 1-prompt's Intent Engine will analyze all your prompts, resolve conflicts (latest instruction wins), remove duplicates, and produce a single unified paragraph.

3
Copy and reuse

Copy the compiled prompt and paste it into any AI platform to recreate your entire conversation context in a single message.

9. Troubleshooting

Extension not capturing prompts

  • Make sure you are on a supported platform.
  • Refresh the page after installing the extension for the first time.
  • Check that the extension is enabled in chrome://extensions.

Side panel not opening

  • Ensure you have Chrome version 116 or later (side panel API requires it).
  • Try clicking the 1-prompt icon in the toolbar, or use the keyboard shortcut.
  • If using a Chromium-based browser (Edge, Brave), ensure side panel support is enabled.

Sign-in not working

  • Make sure you are signed into Chrome with a Google account.
  • Disable other extensions that may block Google OAuth popups.
  • Try clearing the extension's data: right-click the icon > Manage extension > Clear data.

Compilation fails or returns an error

  • Check your internet connection - compilation requires a network request.
  • If you see "Over-Capacity", wait 30 seconds and try again.
  • The extension will automatically fall back to local compilation if the cloud service is unavailable.

10. Uninstalling

  1. Right-click the 1-prompt icon in your toolbar.
  2. Select "Remove from Chrome".
  3. Confirm by clicking "Remove".

This removes the extension and all locally stored data. If you have cloud-synced data and want it deleted, sign in first and use the "Delete All Data" option in the extension settings before uninstalling, or contact us.

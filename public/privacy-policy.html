1. Introduction

1-prompt ("we", "our", "us") is a Chrome browser extension that extracts and compiles user prompts from AI chat platforms. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

By installing and using the 1-prompt extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the extension.

2. Data We Collect

Subscription Info Selected plan tier (Free, Go, Pro) When you create an account

2.3 Telemetry Data (Opt-In Only)

We collect anonymous telemetry data only if you explicitly grant consent. Telemetry is disabled by default. If enabled, we collect:

  • Extension version
  • Feature usage events (e.g., "extraction started", "compilation completed")
  • Error and crash reports

You can enable or disable telemetry at any time from the extension settings. Revoking consent immediately clears any queued telemetry data.

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • AI assistant responses (we only extract your prompts, not the AI's replies)
  • Browsing history or activity outside of supported AI platforms
  • Passwords, financial information, or form data
  • Content from websites other than the supported AI platforms listed below

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all extracted prompts and usage data are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your browser unless you sign in and use cloud features.

4.2 Cloud Storage

When you sign in with Google and use cloud-enabled features (history sync, cross-device access), your data is stored on:

  • Cloudflare Workers KV - Encrypted at rest, hosted on Cloudflare's global edge network

Cloud-stored data includes your user profile, extraction history, and prompts associated with history entries.

4.3 Security Measures

  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication (we never see or store your Google password)
  • Server-side token verification via Google's API
  • API rate limiting (100 requests per minute per IP)
  • Request size limits (1 MB maximum)
  • Input validation and sanitization on all server endpoints
  • CORS restricted to authorized extension origins only

These services process the data transiently and do not retain it after generating the response. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling practices.

7. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until you clear it manually or uninstall the extension.
  • Cloud history: Stored until you delete it via the extension or request account deletion.
  • User profile: Stored as long as your account is active.
  • Telemetry: Aggregated and anonymized; individual events are not retained long-term.
  • Rate limiting data: Automatically expires after 2 minutes.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

8.1 Access and Export

You can view all your extracted prompts and history directly within the extension's side panel.

8.2 Deletion

  • Local data: Clear via Chrome's extension storage settings or by uninstalling the extension.
  • Cloud data: Use the "Clear History" option in the extension, or request complete account data deletion through the extension settings. This deletes your profile, history, and all associated data from our servers.

8.3 Consent Withdrawal

  • Telemetry: Disable at any time from extension settings.
  • Cloud sync: Sign out to stop cloud synchronization.
  • All data processing: Uninstall the extension to stop all data collection.

8.4 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectification, erasure, data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8.5 CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. Children's Privacy

1-prompt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and may notify you via the extension. Your continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

2. Description of Service

1-prompt is a Chrome browser extension that provides two core capabilities:

  • Capture: Extracts your prompts (user messages) from supported AI chat platforms such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and others.
  • Compile: Uses AI to analyze your captured prompts and produce a single, deduplicated, intent-preserving compiled prompt that you can use in any new conversation or LLM.

The Service may also include cloud synchronization, a personal prompt library, and cross-device access for registered users.
